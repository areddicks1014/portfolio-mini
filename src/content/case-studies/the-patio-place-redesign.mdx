<Panel id="project-overview" headerText='Project Overview' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
    <Panel headerText='The Goal' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        I was tasked with redesigning the website to create a modern and mobile-friendly site that directs customers to directly contact the store for sales. The clients wanted the site to have a personality and plenty of color. They also wanted the site to be able to notify customers of new sales, promotions, or updates to their order. Creating a <a class="interactive link" href="https://web.dev/explore/progressive-web-apps" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Progressive Web App <Icon name="mdi:external-link" /></a> allowed me to implement native notifications and for the site to be 'installed' along other apps on the phone for easier access.
    </Panel>
    <Panel headerText='My Roles' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>

        <Panel headerText='UX Designer' headerSize='5' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)' class='inside small'>
            Created wireframes and site maps for the designs.
        </Panel>
        <Panel headerText='UX Researcher' headerSize='5' class='inside small'>
            Conducted user research, competitive analysis, and collected data.
        </Panel>
        <Panel headerText='Web Developer' headerSize='5' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)' class='inside small'>

<Panel id="competitive-analysis" headerText='Competitive Analysis' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
    I sketched out wireframes of the home pages of the competitors and partners who had recently also either rebranded or refreshed their website designs to be more 'modern'.
    <br />
    <Image src="/images/patios-n-stuff/wireframes/Patios-N-Stuff-competitors-wireframes.jpg" alt="Patios-N-Stuff Competitors Wireframes" width="325" height="700" />
    I used <a class="interactive link" href="https://m3.material.io/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Material Design <Icon name="mdi:external-link" /></a> as a starting point since I was using the Polymer framework (which was also created by Google) to build the site with.
</Panel>
<br />

<Panel id="paper-wireframes" headerText='Paper Wireframes' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
    <Panel headerText='Brand Explorations' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        In the original design, I liked how the side menu was curved and gave the site some personality. I wanted to translate that curve into a more modern context by using curved section dividers. I was partially inspired by the Tropitone site, which had a kind of wave-shaped separator for the header and footer. I made the curve shape resemble an umbrella since the logo has an umbrella.
        <br />
        <Image src="/images/patios-n-stuff/wireframes/Patios-N-Stuff-exploration-wireframes.jpg" alt="Patios-N-Stuff Explorations Wireframes" width="325" height="700" /> 
        Important considerations in the new designs was to ensure that the site would be mobile-friendly and would adhere to accessibility standards (at the time) with an appropriate visual hierarchy and color contrast.
    </Panel>
    <Panel headerText='Final Designs' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        The new home page could grow and shrink while maintaining the same visual language of the curved section dividers and the use of primary and secondary colors. I also moved most of the content from the separate pages (About, Brands, Design Services) to be sections on the home page since they did not have a lot of complexity or amount of content.
        <br />
        <div class="panel-grid">
        <Image src="/images/patios-n-stuff/wireframes/Patios-N-Stuff-wireframe-1.jpg" alt="Patios-N-Stuff Wireframe for the Home page" width="325" height="700" /> 
        <Image src="/images/patios-n-stuff/wireframes/Patios-N-Stuff-wireframe-2.jpg" alt="Patios-N-Stuff Wireframe for the Home page" width="325" height="700" /> 
        <Image src="/images/patios-n-stuff/wireframes/Patios-N-Stuff-wireframe-3.jpg" alt="Patios-N-Stuff Wireframe for the Product and Collections pages" width="325" height="700" /> 
        </div>
        I added some call-to-action buttons for connecting the user to somebody at the actual store so that they could best serve their customers' needs as well as a way to draw in new customers with a coupon offer.
    </Panel>
</Panel>

<Panel id='what-i-learned' headerText='What I Learned' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
<div class='panel-grid'>
        <Panel headerText='Client Taste' headerSize='4' class='inside small'>
            Some clients may not know what they really want until they see it and will change the scope given the opportunity.
        </Panel>
        <Panel headerText='Rapid Iteration' headerSize='4' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)' class='inside small'>
            Being able to rapidly iterate with the client is important for keeping the momentum going for the project.
        </Panel>
        <Panel headerText='Communication Is Key' headerSize='4' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)' class='inside small'>
            Keeping an open channel of communication for questions and feedback was helpful for preventing unnecessary work further in the process.
        </Panel>
    </div>
</Panel>
