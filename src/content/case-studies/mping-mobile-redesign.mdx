<Panel id="project-overview" headerText='Project Overview' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
    <Panel headerText='The Goal' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        The mPING (Meteorological Phenomena Identification Near the Ground) mobile apps on Android + iOS have outdated designs that could also be confusing for new users.
    </Panel>
    <Panel headerText='My Roles' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
    <div class='panel-grid'>
        <Panel headerText='UX Designer' headerSize='5' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)' class='inside small'>
            Created wireframes, mockups, and prototypes for the redesigns.
        </Panel>
        <Panel headerText='Web Developer' headerSize='5' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)' class='inside small'>
            Created a <a class="interactive link" href="https://web.dev/explore/progressive-web-apps" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Progressive Web App <Icon name="mdi:external-link" /></a> that would work across platforms that could also serve as a high fidelity functional prototype that uses the APIs provided by NOAA + NSSL + OU.
        </Panel>
    </div>
    </Panel>
    <Panel headerText='Context' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        - I have been using this app for many years casually and while storm chasing
        - The design has degraded over time as devices and operating systems have been updated
        - This functionality has been built into other apps (such as RadarScope)
    </Panel>
</Panel>
