            </Panel>
            <Panel headerText='Scheduling' headerSize='5' class='inside small'>

            </Panel>
            <Panel headerText='Care' headerSize='5' class='inside small'>
                Knowing when and how to care for each plant can be stressful if any of the plants have special needs or need medicine.

<Panel id="digital-wireframes" headerText='Digital Wireframes' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
    <Panel headerText='Home page' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        The Home page is meant to be simple, not too busy, and helpful for taking care of your houseplants.
        <br />
        <Image src="/images/wireframes/panacea-digital-wireframe-home.png" alt="Panacea Digital Wireframe for the Home page" width="325" height="700" /> 
        The top section is for plants that need care or attention today and the plant can be selected to view plant details directly. The search button at the bottom right brings up bottom modal with choice of media picker, camera, or text to search for plant type or ailment.
    </Panel>
    <Panel headerText='All Plants page' headerSize='4' class='inside' backgroundColor='var(--gray-999-rgb)'>
        When going from one page to another, I wanted the controls placement to remain consistent. For example, the 'add', 'edit', and 'save' buttons are in the same place on the right side.
        <br />
        <Image src="/images/wireframes/panacea-digital-wireframe-all-plants.png" alt="Panacea Digital Wireframe for the All Plants page" width="325" height="700" />
        <Image src="/images/wireframes/panacea-digital-wireframe-plant-details.png" alt="Panacea Digital Wireframe for the Plant Details page" width="325" height="700" />
        Select a plant from the list to see its details.
        Important controls are always at the bottom, easily within reach while holding a phone with one hand. 
    </Panel>
</Panel>
