<Panel id="description" headerText='Description' headerSize='3' backgroundColor='var(--color-sm-miles-skill-tree-hd-primary-rgb)'>
    The Skill Tree design from Insomniac's Spider-Man Miles Morales game for PS4/5 is so good that I couldn't resist making a more...portable version. 
    <br />
    The stats (starting from the bottom left and going clockwise):
    - Battery (🔋)
        - The battery icon will turn red when at or below 25% and green while charging
    - Calories (🔥)
    - Steps (👟)
    - Active Zone Minutes (⬆️)
    - Heart Rate (💛🧡❤️)
        - The heart will change color to correspond with the current Active Zone to match Fitbit's colors.
    <br />
    All the credit goes to the amazing work done by Gil (<a class="interactive link" href="https://twitter.com/GilFromUI" target="_blank" rel="noopener">@GilFromUI <Icon name="mdi:external-link" /></a> on Twitter) and the Insomniac team on the design work that I was inspired by.
</Panel>
